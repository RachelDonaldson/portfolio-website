My Manifesto

Concept Ideation
Illustration
Typography

Overview

My manifesto is a battle cry to inspire me when things get tough. The goal was to develop a typographic piece that would inspire me to persevere and that would capture my ethos as a designer.


Problem

It can be easy to get discouraged as a designer and to over-look the little step towards progress. There will be lots obstacles ahead and plenty of learning moments. Therefore, I wanted to create I visual reminder of what really matters to help me along the way.

Solution

I have created an illustrative and typographic piece stating “It’s the process that’s important”. This will reminder when I’m struggling to find solutions to problems or come up with that brilliant concept that it’s the struggle that I’m learning from not the end result. I should always be grateful for the progress I’m making no matter how small it seems.
